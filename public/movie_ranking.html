My Favourite Gentle Movies

Sometimes a movie should be a warm embrace, soothing you after a tiring day; or offering comfort in times of sickness and emotional distress.

These are my top three.

Howl's Moving Castle

I watch this whenever I am hungover.

The Princess Bride

The chicken soup of films.

Labyrinth

My favourite film of all time. It connects me to my childhood, I appreciate the puppetry. David Bowie. Goblins...

I love it.

I watch it when I feel sad, I watch it when I want to feel brave, I watch it when I want to reconnect to my inner child.

Also, David Bowie.
